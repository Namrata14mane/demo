About Us

Mangalmurti Infra Space Creators Pvt. Ltd. is a trusted real estate developer focused on affordable housing. Registered under the Companies Act, headquartered in Thane, we specialize in PMAY (Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana) projects.

Our commitment is to provide affordable, quality housing that supports the dream of home ownership for low-income families.

Vision & Mission

  • Vision:To be a leading name in affordable housing, creating inclusive communities that uplift families and support national housing goals.
  • Vision:To provide affordable, quality housing to low-income families, enabling home ownership in Navi Mumbai.

Our Project

  • Target buyers: Low-income groups
  • Project type: Affordable housing under PMAY
  • Price: ₹25 lakh per unit
  • Unit size: 420 sq.ft. carpet area
  • Eco-friendly, sustainable design
  • Subsidy assistance for eligible buyers
  • Total Units: 1,42,708 units

About PMAY

  • Launched in 2015 by Government of India
  • Goal: Housing for All – Affordable homes for urban poor
  • Credit-linked subsidy on home loans (CLSS)
  • Focus on EWS, LIG, MIG segments
  • Preference to women, senior citizens, and differently-abled

Why Choose Us?

  • Hassle-free loan & subsidy documentation assistance
